Not Your Regular Snack

Simple Ingredients

We believe in using ingredients you can pronounce, that means no artificial preservatives. All our snacks are hand-made in small batches, freshness guaranteed. We put love and care into each bite, from when our ingredients are chosen to the moment each crisp is sliced, each cookie is made...up until the instant each bag is ripped open and enjoyed by you.

Buying SLICED = Supporting a women-owned Asian business

We are proudly women-run and owned by two generations of immigrants. Our team makes SLICED snacks in Virginia, and while one of our goals is to bring you delicious and authentic snacks, we are also active in our Lao and Thai communities giving back to the community that helped our business grow.
